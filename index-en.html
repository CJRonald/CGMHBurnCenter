About Us

Linkou CGMH Burn Center was founded in 1978 by Dr. Samuel Noordhoff. Initially, it had 20 beds and was the largest burn unit in Taiwan. Now, the center has expanded to 30 beds, including a dedicated ICU and four attending physicians. The center features 21 acute intensive care isolation beds, 9 subacute burn beds, 2 independent operating rooms, a hydrotherapy and dressing room, 3 sand beds, and 3 air beds. Each bed is equipped with emergency equipment, monitors, and intercoms; every room has a ceiling-mounted TV, calendar, clock, and bathroom, providing patients with the best care environment.

The center holds regular multidisciplinary team meetings every week, involving physicians, nurses, nutritionists, physical therapists, occupational therapists, respiratory therapists, and social workers, and provides health education for families. In addition, we collaborate with foundations such as the Sunshine Foundation to hold seminars, offering comprehensive support for patients and families. Linkou CGMH Burn Center is currently the largest and best-equipped burn center in Taiwan, dedicated to providing high-quality, holistic burn care.

Service

Four Core Services of Comprehensive Burn Care

Acute Burn Intensive Care

24-hour dedicated burn care team, advanced ICU and emergency facilities, from on-site rescue and emergency management to inpatient care, providing comprehensive, immediate, and high-quality critical care for burn patients.

Scar Reconstruction

Personalized reconstructive surgery, laser therapy, and rehabilitation programs for burn sequelae and chronic scars, helping patients improve appearance and function, and enhance quality of life and confidence.

Oculonasal Reconstruction

Specializing in eye, nose, and facial plastic and reconstructive surgery, combining advanced surgical techniques and aesthetics to restore function and natural appearance, rebuilding confidence and social ability.

Hand and Joint Reconstruction

For hand burns, trauma, or joint contractures, we provide microsurgery, joint reconstruction, and functional rehabilitation to help patients regain hand mobility and independence in daily life.

Research

Covering all important aspects of burn medicine

Visual-driven Burn Analysis and Prediction

Integrating AI and medical imaging analysis to develop automated burn area and depth recognition systems, assisting physicians in rapid and accurate assessment, predicting treatment outcomes, and enhancing clinical decision-making and care quality.

3D-Printed Nasal Reconstruction

Utilizing 3D printing and personalized medical design to create custom nasal reconstruction solutions for patients with defects or deformities, combining precision surgery and aesthetics to restore nasal appearance and function, and improve confidence and quality of life.

Customized Finger Extension Device Reconstruction

For hand burns or trauma causing extension dysfunction, we develop customized extension devices and reconstructive surgery, combined with rehabilitation training, to help patients regain hand mobility and independence in daily life.
